Chat Data Storage

The JukeBOT feature within our application saves chat conversations locally on Your Device. This includes:

  • Chat messages exchanged with JukeBOT
  • Chat titles and timestamps
  • Message history and conversation flow

Important: All chat data is stored exclusively on Your Device using the local storage functionality of Your browser or mobile device. We do not transmit, store, or process chat conversations on our servers.

Chat data remains private to You and is not shared with the Company, service providers, or any third parties.

JukeShorts Video Content

The JukeShorts feature allows you to upload and share short-form video content. When you use JukeShorts, we process:

  • Video files: Uploaded videos (maximum 700MB per file, maximum 60 seconds duration)
  • Thumbnail images: Custom preview images (maximum 20MB per image)
  • Video metadata: Title, description, category, hashtags, duration information
  • Technical data: Video resolution, format, file size, encoding information
  • Upload analytics: Timestamps, IP address during upload, processing status

Processing: Uploaded videos undergo automatic optimization (transcoding and compression) to ensure optimal playback performance across devices. Original files may be temporarily stored during processing but are replaced by optimized versions.

Storage: Processed videos and thumbnails are stored on our secure servers until you choose to delete them or your account is closed.

QR Code and PDF Generation Data

The QR Code generation and management features process the following data:

  • QR Session Data: Unique session identifiers (sessionId, qrCodeId), creation timestamps, activation status, expiration dates
  • Event Configuration Data: Selected language, playlist preferences, custom images, social media links configured for the public page
  • PDF Generation Data: Configuration settings used to generate printable PDF documents containing QR codes and event information
  • Public URL Data: Generated URLs that point to your public request page, containing session identifiers for tracking purposes

Processing: This data is used exclusively to:

  • Generate unique QR codes for your events
  • Create printable PDF documents in various formats (complete PDF or QR-only)
  • Manage 24-hour activation windows for QR code validity
  • Associate song requests with the correct event session

Important: Generated PDF documents are:

  • Created locally in your browser and downloaded directly to your device
  • Not stored on our servers or cloud storage
  • Contain session identifiers but no personally identifiable email information
  • Available in two formats: Complete PDF (with instructions and branding) or QR-only PDF (minimal format)

QR Code Content: QR codes contain a URL with a session identifier that points to your public request page. This URL does not contain email addresses or other personal identifiers, only the session ID needed to associate requests with your event.

Account Verification Data

To ensure platform security and prevent abuse, we implement an account verification system. When you request to activate QR codes, we collect:

  • Verification Codes: 8-character alphanumeric codes generated for one-time use
  • Session Data: Unique verification session IDs, timestamps of code generation
  • Verification Status: Current verification state (unverified, pending, verified)
  • Attempt Logs: Timestamps and IP addresses for verification attempts
  • Expiration Data: Code validity period (10 minutes from generation)

Purpose: Account verification is required to:

  • Prevent bot activity and automated account creation
  • Limit spam and misuse of QR code activation features
  • Ensure service quality for all legitimate users
  • Comply with anti-abuse regulations and platform security standards

Processing: Verification codes are:

  • Generated automatically and sent to your registered email address
  • Valid for exactly 10 minutes from creation
  • Deleted immediately after successful verification or expiration
  • Limited to 3 attempt failures before requiring a new code

Manual Entry Requirement: For enhanced security, verification codes must be manually typed. Copy-paste functionality is disabled to prevent automated verification attempts.
