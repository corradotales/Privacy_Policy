Payment Data and Credits

When you purchase Credits via Stripe, we collect the following data:

Chat Data Storage

The JukeBOT feature within our application saves chat conversations locally on Your Device. This includes:

  • Chat messages exchanged with JukeBOT
  • Chat titles and timestamps
  • Message history and conversation flow

Important: All chat data is stored exclusively on Your Device using the local storage functionality of Your browser or mobile device. We do not transmit, store, or process chat conversations on our servers.

The application retains up to 5 most recent chats. You can:

  • View chat history in the dedicated chat section
  • Delete individual chats using the delete functionality
  • Permanently remove all chat data by clearing the application data from Your Device settings

Chat data remains private to You and is not shared with the Company, service providers, or any third parties.

JukeShorts Video Content

The JukeShorts feature allows you to upload and share short-form video content. When you use JukeShorts, we process:

  • Video files: Uploaded videos (maximum 700MB per file, maximum 60 seconds duration)
  • Thumbnail images: Custom preview images (maximum 20MB per image)
  • Video metadata: Title, description, category, hashtags, duration information
  • Technical data: Video resolution, format, file size, encoding information
  • Upload analytics: Timestamps, IP address during upload, processing status

Processing: Uploaded videos undergo automatic optimization (transcoding and compression) to ensure optimal playback performance across devices. Original files may be temporarily stored during processing but are replaced by optimized versions.

Storage: Processed videos and thumbnails are stored on our secure servers until you choose to delete them or your account is closed.

QR Code and PDF Generation Data

The QR Code generation and management features process the following data:

  • QR Session Data: Unique session identifiers (sessionId, qrCodeId), creation timestamps, activation status, expiration dates
  • Event Configuration Data: Selected language, playlist preferences, custom images, social media links configured for the public page
  • PDF Generation Data: Configuration settings used to generate printable PDF documents containing QR codes and event information
  • Public URL Data: Generated URLs that point to your public request page, containing session identifiers for tracking purposes

Processing: This data is used exclusively to:

  • Generate unique QR codes for your events
  • Create printable PDF documents in various formats (complete PDF or QR-only)
  • Manage 24-hour activation windows for QR code validity
  • Associate song requests with the correct event session

Important: Generated PDF documents are:

  • Created locally in your browser and downloaded directly to your device
  • Not stored on our servers or cloud storage
  • Contain session identifiers but no personally identifiable email information
  • Available in two formats: Complete PDF (with instructions and branding) or QR-only PDF (minimal format)

QR Code Content: QR codes contain a URL with a session identifier that points to your public request page. This URL does not contain email addresses or other personal identifiers, only the session ID needed to associate requests with your event.

Use of Chat Data

Locally stored chat data is used solely to:

  • Provide continuity in Your conversations with JukeBOT
  • Allow You to review previous chat history
  • Enhance Your user experience within the application

Chat data is not used for:

  • Analytics or tracking
  • Machine learning model training
  • Marketing purposes
  • User profiling
  • Transaction ID: Unique identifier generated by Stripe to validate the purchase and resolve disputes.
  • Consent Data: Timestamp, consent version, IP address, user agent, plan type (planType), and priceId. This data proves your explicit acceptance of the non-refundable nature of the service (Art. 59 Italian Consumer Code).

Use of JukeShorts Content

Video content uploaded to JukeShorts is used to:

  • Provide and display your video content within the JukeShorts platform
  • Optimize videos for streaming across different devices and network conditions
  • Apply content moderation and community guidelines
  • Generate preview thumbnails for video discovery
  • Calculate and enforce upload limits (daily and total upload restrictions)
  • Prevent abuse through IP-based rate limiting

Content Visibility: By default, videos uploaded to JukeShorts are publicly accessible unless otherwise specified in future privacy settings.

Moderation: All videos undergo review before publication to ensure compliance with our community guidelines. This process typically completes within 48 hours.

Use of QR Code and PDF Data

QR Code session data and configuration settings are used exclusively to:

  • Generate unique, scannable QR codes for your musical events
  • Create printable PDF documents containing:
    • Complete PDF: QR code, event details, instructions, and JukeBox branding
    • QR-only PDF: Minimal format with just the QR code in various sizes (small, medium, large)
  • Manage time-limited QR code activation (24-hour validity periods)
  • Track and associate song requests with the correct event session
  • Provide public access pages configured with your selected language, playlist, and social links

Local Processing: All PDF generation occurs locally in your web browser. Configuration data is transmitted to generate the QR code, but the final PDF document is created and downloaded directly to your device without server-side storage.

Data Minimization: QR codes contain only the minimum necessary information (session ID) to connect to your event. No email addresses, names, or other personal identifiers are encoded in the QR code itself.

Session Lifespan: QR session data is automatically purged after the session expires (24 hours after activation) plus a 30-day grace period for analytics.

Note: We do not store sensitive payment details (e.g., card numbers, CVV). All payments are processed by Stripe Payments Europe Ltd.
